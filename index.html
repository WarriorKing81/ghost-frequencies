GHOST FREQUENCY

This experience requires headphones.

Put them on before continuing.

BEFORE YOU ENTER:

Stay quiet. Speak softly. The Haunted One is listening.

Loud noises, yelling, or screaming will draw it out.

Stay off the red zone or it will find you.

This app uses your microphone and camera. The mic detects noise levels so the ghost can hear you. The camera captures your reactions during jump scares. No data is sent or stored anywhere.
